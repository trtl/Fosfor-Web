SUBSTANCES SHOWCASE

AUDITORIJA . KAUNAS

As the Earth completes another successful orbit around the Sun, it’s time to welcome a new cycle. The eve of the second SUBSTANCES creative competition is not only a hint of what’s yet to come, but also an experiment in its own right. This night will become an experimental space for sonic performance, where genres are not fixed and the boundaries between EBM and electro are deliberately pushed. For one night only, FOSFOR residents arrive to illuminate both unexplored and long- forgotten corners of electronic music. The program features an intense live performance alongside a spectrum of unconventional selections, stretching from slow and viscous to fast and uplifting. Joining the collective under an alternate alias is last year’s SUBSTANCES competition prizewinner and jury favorite – Integra.
