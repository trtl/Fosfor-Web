WHAT IS SUBSTANCES?

TRACK PRODUCTION CONTEST CREATED WITH INTENT

SUBSTANCES is a collection of sounds for those unafraid to explore and experiment. Textures, layers, and rhythms form a foundation for your creative ideas, giving shape to the concepts spinning in your mind. Curated by KabaharT and produced along with fellow sound fanatics, the pack blends raw energy with meticulous detail. Every hit, one-shot, and loop is designed to inspire, challenge, and push your sonic boundaries. More than a pack, SUBSTANCES is a platform for emerging producers – a test, a challenge, and an opportunity to face fear, give it your all, and release your unshackled self into the world. Through contests, collaborations, and live showcases, Fosfor supports creators in turning experimentation into fully realized tracks, performances, and releases. Dive in, create, and let sound speak what words cannot.

The creative contest put simply

The creative contest put simply Lithuania’s electronic music scene has been inspiring us for a long time, and now’s the time to give something back. Not only are we releasing our first sample pack for free, but we‘re also inviting you to take part in our creative contest – SUBSTANCES. The course is simple: • Download the sample pack from our bio • Create an original 3–5 min. track following the provided guidelines • Send it to us – your creation will be evaluated by our jury and listeners Send the track over to label.fosfor@gmail.com by May 1st. The best tracks will be featured on our SoundCloud, and those with the most support will receive additional prizes from us and our sponsors. Show us what form sound can take.
